What is UV?

Ultraviolet (UV) radiation is generated from the sun and enters our atmosphere. Overexposure to UV can cause damage to skin cells and lead to skin cancer.

What is skin cancer?

Skin cancer can be caused by exposure to ultraviolet radiation, most commonly from the sun. Every time our skin is exposed to ultraviolet radiation, the structure of our skin cells changes.
